Finish & linger — the finished book doesn't vanish

Tapping I finished the book on the log sheet marks it finished but keeps the card on Currently Reading until the next app open — no forced review, no jarring disappear. The card wears a quiet gold "done" treatment and offers a dismissible "rate & review?" nudge. Undo-finish and Rate & review live in the same ⋯ menu that holds Pause / DNF / Add-format — so the sheet stays clean.

The through-line: finishing is a soft landing, not a trapdoor. The book stays put for the rest of the session (state 1), you can rate it or take it back with Undo finish from ⋯ (state 2), and once you dismiss the nudge it simply waits to graduate to Library (state 3).  ·  Finish is triggered from the log sheet's "I finished the book" secondary action — everything here is the aftermath.
