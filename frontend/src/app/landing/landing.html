What sets it apart

Two problems most trackers ignore

Designed · in progress

Switch formats mid-book, re-read on purpose

Most trackers treat a re-listen or a format swap as a new book. Here, one "read" can move between audio, ebook, and print, and re-reading a chapter you already covered logs as time spent without inflating your completion percent.

Designed · in progress

Anthologies and omnibuses, story by story

Read one short story from a collection, or one issue from an omnibus, out of order, without tracking the whole book or wrecking your percent complete. Contents are delineated, so progress is always the truth.
